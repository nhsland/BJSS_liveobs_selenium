Recently Discharged / Transferred Pages
=======================================
The Recently Discharged and Recently Transferred pages show the user patients
who have been discharged or transferred from the ward they are allocated to in the
last 72 (by default) hours.

The number of hours that records are shown for is configurable by the System Administrator.
